How I got into Eminem

I first started listening to eminem without actually knowing it. My favorite songs when I was younger were "Not Afraid" and "Love the Way you Lie", but I had no idea who wrote them. When I was 12, "Monster" came out and I recognized the voice, and from there I became obsessed with eminem's music. Some of my favorite things about eminiem include: his vocabulary, his ability to rhyme words like symbiote and orange, and the way he can tell a story through rhymes, like in "Like Toy Soldiers" and "Stan". I'm also a fan of his fast verses, like in "speedom", "godzilla", or "Rap God". I find that eminem's music covers a wide range of topics, and has a place on just about any playlist.
